About Me

This is what I do.

Born and raised in Las Vegas, Nevada, I'm a tried and true desert rat. Now a Utah transplant attending school at Brigham Young University, I find myself relishing in greener hills, mountain sides, and trails. It seems popular around here to complain about Utah, but I love it. Provo's proximity to the mountains makes this place prime real estate.

Im the youngest of 4 brothers, proud funcle to 3 nephews (the third not pictured here), and the son of the most classically named parents ever: Gary and Karen.

I spend my days studying and working, with the hopes of one day having a meaningful career in either business or the United States intelligence community.
